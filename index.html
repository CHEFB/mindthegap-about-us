About Us

Bighani, Cecilia, Hannah, Erin, and Francesca are high school students. We spent the summer of 2016 at the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program hosted at Disney! Girls Who Code is a nonprofit organization that tries to increase the number of women involved in technology by inspiring high school girls to love computer science.
For our final project, we found a topic that we were all passionate about. As young women on the cusp of joining the workforce, we all have personal interest in trying to close the wage gap. We learned that on average, women make only 79% as much as men do over their lifetimes. We wanted to do our part by working to increase awareness of this issue and that will help to eventually establish equal pay. This inspired us to create our game, Mind The Gap. We hope you enjoy.
